5 Reasons Why Deep Learning Works
Speaker:
Mattia Cerrato
Why does Deep Learning work at all? Classical ML theory does not really say, as the models are grossly overparametrized and there is a lot of "art" involved in obtaining the best architecture and hyperparameters for a given task. While a definite answer is not here yet, the research community does have some answers already. This presentation tries to discuss a few of these while maintaining a critical approach.
