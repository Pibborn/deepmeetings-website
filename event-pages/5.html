Intro to Capsule Networks
Speaker:
Mattia Cerrato
Follow-up work by Sabour et al. [1] about Capsule Networks has attracted considerable attention by the research community. To understand it properly, one first has to discuss the original work by Hinton et al. [2]. In this installment we discuss the Transforming Auto-Encoder concept and how it might lead to the improvement of more commonly used deep networks.
