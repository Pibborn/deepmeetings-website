Limits of Cognitive Science-Inspired Interpretation Approaches<
Speaker:
Antonio Lieto
Recently, researchers at the intersection of AI and Cognitive Science have been interested in using techniques borrowed from Cognitive Psychology to interpret and understand how Deep Nets work. However, this approach relies on the still very discussed assumption that deep networks and the human brain have enough in common for the aforementioned techniques to hold. In this presentation we focused on a specific paper [1] coming from this line of thinking.
