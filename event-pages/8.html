Atoms of Recognition in Human and Computer Vision
Speaker:
Enrico Mensa
The similarities between deep CNNs and the human visive system have attracted both fascination and criticism from computer and cognitive scientists. The presented work by Ullman et al. uses the concept of "minimal images" to investigate the differences between how convolutional networks and humans recognize objects in images.
